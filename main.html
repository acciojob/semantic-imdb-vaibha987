Summary

Fantastic Beasts and Where to Find Them is a fantasy film set in the wizarding world of Harry Potter. It follows Newt Scamander, a magizoologist, as he arrives in 1926 New York City with a suitcase full of magical creatures. Chaos ensues when some of the creatures escape, and Newt must recover them while navigating the magical and non-magical tensions of the time.

Actors

Eddie Redmayne stars as Newt Scamander, the quirky and kind-hearted magizoologist.

Katherine Waterston portrays Tina Goldstein, a determined witch working for the Magical Congress of the United States of America (MACUSA).

Dan Fogler plays Jacob Kowalski, a No-Maj (non-magical person) who gets caught up in Newt's adventures.
